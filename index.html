"Welcome, Poets All!"

ALLAMA IQBAL DAY
POETRY FESTIVAL 2021

On the 9th of November, on this great man's birthday, poets and philosphers from the entire nation gather every year at Lahore to commemmorate him and follow in his footsteps.
This year, we have invited the foremost poets of this age, who are presenting their own modern interperations of his age-old, yet ageless verses, in light of current events.
Besides poetry recitals, we also intend to have a section on the philosophical and contextual meaning of a selection of Allama Muhammad Iqbal's poetry himself.
Everyone interested should feel free to apply for a spot on our programme.

Please contact us via email for any further questions about Iqbal Day Poetry Festival 2021!
